# Where to Stay Near San Raffaele Hospital in Milan

Practical advice for patients, relatives, university visitors and professionals looking for comfortable accommodation with convenient public transport connections.

Travelling to Milan for a hospital appointment, treatment or to support a relative creates different accommodation needs from an ordinary holiday.

Comfort, flexibility and access to everyday services often matter more than staying close to tourist attractions.

This guide explains how to choose accommodation near San Raffaele Hospital and what to consider before booking.

## Where is San Raffaele Hospital?

San Raffaele Hospital is located in the eastern part of Milan, in Via Olgettina.

The hospital complex also includes medical research facilities and Vita-Salute San Raffaele University.

It is outside the historic centre but can be reached using Milan's public transport network.

The usual public transport route involves reaching Cascina Gobba on the M2 Green Line and continuing to the hospital by the dedicated light metro or local bus.

## How to reach San Raffaele by public transport

The main public transport connection is through:

**Cascina Gobba M2**

From Cascina Gobba, visitors can continue towards the hospital using:

- The dedicated San Raffaele light metro.
- ATM bus 925.
- Other connections available for the chosen travel date.

The dedicated light metro departs from the Cascina Gobba station area and connects directly with the hospital campus.

Always check current schedules before travelling, particularly during weekends, holidays or maintenance periods.

If you're planning to rely entirely on public transport during your stay, you may also find our guide to [visiting Milan without a car](/guides/where-to-stay-in-milan-without-a-car) useful.

## Can you reach San Raffaele from Sesto San Giovanni?

Yes.

From areas served by the M1 Red Line, the journey generally requires a change to the M2 Green Line before continuing to Cascina Gobba.

A typical route may involve:

1. Taking the M1 towards central Milan.
2. Changing to the M2 Green Line.
3. Travelling to Cascina Gobba.
4. Continuing by the dedicated light metro or bus 925.

The exact journey depends on the departure station, service conditions and time of day.

For hospital visits, it is sensible to leave additional time in case of waiting periods or service changes.

## Why an apartment can be useful for a hospital stay

Hospital-related visits may last longer than expected.

An apartment can provide greater flexibility than a traditional hotel room, particularly for patients and relatives staying several days.

Useful features may include:

- A fully equipped kitchen.
- A washing machine.
- Separate living and sleeping areas.
- Wi-Fi.
- Self check-in.
- Supermarkets and pharmacies nearby.
- Space to rest between hospital visits.

The possibility of preparing simple meals can be especially valuable during longer or medically demanding stays.

## What to look for when choosing accommodation

When comparing accommodation, consider more than the straight-line distance from the hospital.

Check:

- The complete public transport route.
- The number of changes required.
- Walking distance to the nearest metro station.
- Early-morning and evening transport options.
- Supermarkets and pharmacies nearby.
- Flexible check-in.
- Cancellation and date-change conditions.
- Whether the accommodation is suitable for a longer stay.

A slightly more distant apartment with a clear transport connection may be more practical than accommodation that appears close on a map but is difficult to reach.

## Staying in a residential area

A residential area can offer important advantages during a hospital-related stay.

These may include:

- Quieter evenings.
- Access to supermarkets.
- Local cafés and restaurants.
- Pharmacies and everyday services.
- More spacious accommodation.
- A less crowded environment than central Milan.

For relatives accompanying a patient, having a comfortable place to rest can make a demanding visit more manageable.

## Arriving from Milan's airports

Public transport options vary according to the airport.

### Linate Airport

Bus 923 provides a connection between Linate and San Raffaele Hospital.

### Malpensa Airport

Visitors can travel towards Milan by train or airport coach and continue using the metro network.

### Bergamo Orio al Serio Airport

Airport coaches connect with Milan and other transport hubs, from which visitors can continue towards Cascina Gobba.

Always check the complete route before departure, particularly when travelling with luggage or arriving late.

## Local tip

> Plan the entire door-to-door journey, not only the metro section.

Before booking, check:

- The walk from the apartment to the metro.
- The required interchange.
- The connection from Cascina Gobba to the hospital.
- The return route after the appointment.
- Weekend or holiday schedules.

Saving the route on your phone before travelling can make hospital days considerably less stressful.
