# Where to Stay Near the M1 Red Line

If you're planning a trip to Milan, staying close to the M1 Red Line can make getting around the city much easier.

Rather than choosing accommodation based only on its distance from the city centre, it's worth considering how easily you can reach the places you want to visit.

The M1 is one of Milan's main metro lines and connects several of the city's most important destinations without the need to change trains.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide may be helpful if you're:

- Visiting Milan for the first time.
- Travelling without a car.
- Looking for a quieter place to stay while remaining well connected.
- Visiting friends or family in northern Milan.
- Planning to combine sightseeing with business or events.

## Why stay near the M1 Red Line?

The M1 is one of the most useful metro lines for visitors because it connects many of Milan's best-known areas using a single route.

Staying within walking distance of an M1 station means you can often reach your destination without changing lines, making daily travel simpler and more predictable.

For many visitors, that's more valuable than staying in the city centre itself.

## Why Sesto San Giovanni is worth considering

Sesto San Giovanni sits at the northern end of the M1 Red Line and offers an excellent combination of convenience and everyday comfort.

Many visitors choose the area because it provides easy access to central Milan while offering a quieter residential atmosphere.

You'll also find everything you may need during your stay, including:

- Supermarkets.
- Pharmacies.
- Cafés.
- Restaurants.
- Bakeries.
- Local shops.

For many travellers, this makes daily life easier than staying in a busier part of the city.

## More than Milan
