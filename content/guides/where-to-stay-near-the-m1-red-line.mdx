# Where to Stay Near the M1 Red Line

Choosing accommodation near the M1 Red Line is one of the easiest ways to make your stay in Milan more convenient.

Whether you're visiting the city's historic centre, attending a business event or exploring different neighbourhoods, staying close to a direct metro line can save time and make getting around much simpler.

For many visitors, the quality of the transport connection matters more than staying in the city centre itself.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide may be helpful if you're:

- Visiting Milan for the first time.
- Travelling without a car.
- Looking for a quieter place to stay while remaining well connected.
- Visiting friends or family in northern Milan.
- Attending a business event or exhibition.
- Planning to explore both Milan and nearby destinations.

## Why choose accommodation near the M1 Red Line?

The M1 Red Line is one of Milan's most important metro lines and connects many of the places visitors travel to most often.

Staying close to one of its stations means you can often reach your destination without changing lines, making journeys simpler and more predictable.

Instead of focusing only on the distance between your accommodation and the city centre, it's worth thinking about how easily you can reach the places you'll actually visit.

A well-connected location can help you spend less time travelling and more time enjoying your stay.

## Why Sesto San Giovanni is worth considering

Sesto San Giovanni is located at the northern end of the M1 Red Line and offers an excellent balance between convenience and everyday comfort.

Many visitors choose the area because it combines easy access to Milan with a quieter residential atmosphere.

From here, you can reach the city centre directly on the M1 while enjoying a neighbourhood with all the services most travellers need during their stay.

You'll find:

- Supermarkets.
- Pharmacies.
- Cafés.
- Restaurants.
- Bakeries.
- Local shops.

For many guests, staying in a residential area also means a more relaxed experience after a busy day in Milan.

## More than Milan

The M1 is only part of the picture.

Sesto San Giovanni is also served by regional railway connections, making it easy to explore destinations beyond Milan without renting a car.

For visitors staying several days, this makes Sesto San Giovanni a practical base for discovering more of northern Italy.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is the M1 Red Line good for tourists?

Yes. It connects several of Milan's most popular destinations, including Duomo, San Babila, Cadorna and Rho Fiera Milano.

### Is staying near the M1 better than staying in the city centre?

For many visitors, it can be. A well-connected location often provides easier journeys, a quieter environment and better value than accommodation in the busiest central areas.

### Can I visit Milan without a car if I stay near the M1?

Absolutely. The M1 provides convenient access to many of the city's main attractions and connects with the rest of Milan's public transport network.

### Is Sesto San Giovanni connected to the M1?

Yes. Several M1 stations serve Sesto San Giovanni, making it easy to travel directly to central Milan.

### Is the M1 useful for business travellers?

Yes. In addition to the city centre, the line provides direct access to destinations such as Rho Fiera Milano, making it a practical choice for many business visitors.
