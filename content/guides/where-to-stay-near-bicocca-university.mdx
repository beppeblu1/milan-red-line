# Where to Stay Near Bicocca University

If you're visiting the University of Milano-Bicocca, choosing the right place to stay can make your trip simpler and more enjoyable.

Many visitors start by looking for accommodation as close to the campus as possible. While this might seem like the obvious choice, it's not always the most practical one.

In many cases, staying in a well-connected residential area gives you greater flexibility, a quieter environment and easy access not only to the university, but also to the rest of Milan.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide may be helpful if you're:

- Visiting a student, friend or family member.
- Attending a conference, seminar or graduation ceremony.
- Working with the university.
- Looking for an apartment instead of a hotel.
- Planning to combine your university visit with a few days exploring Milan.

## About the University of Milano-Bicocca

The University of Milano-Bicocca is one of Italy's leading public universities, welcoming thousands of students, researchers and international visitors every year.

The campus is located in the north-east of Milan and is well connected by public transport. Because of this, many visitors choose accommodation outside the immediate university area without sacrificing convenience.

Many researchers, lecturers and conference attendees also choose accommodation with convenient public transport rather than staying next to the campus. If you're travelling for work, you may also find our guide to the [best areas to stay in Milan for business travellers](/guides/best-area-to-stay-in-milan-for-business-travellers) useful.

Rather than focusing only on distance, it's worth considering how easily you can reach the campus each day.

## What to look for when choosing accommodation

A comfortable stay depends on more than just being close to the university.

When comparing different areas, look for:

- Easy access to public transport.
- Supermarkets and pharmacies nearby.
- Restaurants and cafés within walking distance.
- A quiet neighbourhood where you can relax after a busy day.
- Straightforward connections to other parts of Milan.

A direct journey is often more convenient than a shorter route that requires multiple transport changes.

## Why Sesto San Giovanni is worth considering

Sesto San Giovanni is a residential area just north of Milan that offers an excellent balance between convenience and comfort.

Visitors staying here benefit from easy access to the M1 Red Line, local railway services and a wide range of everyday amenities.

If you're not familiar with the area, you may also like our guide to [staying in Sesto San Giovanni](/guides/is-sesto-san-giovanni-a-good-place-to-stay), which explains why many visitors choose it as a base for exploring Milan.

Being close to the [M1 Red Line](/guides/where-to-stay-near-the-m1-red-line) also makes travelling around the city quick and straightforward.

The area offers:

- Supermarkets.
- Pharmacies.
- Bakeries.
- Cafés.
- Restaurants.
- Local shops and essential services.

Many guests appreciate returning to a quieter neighbourhood after spending the day at the university or exploring the city.

## Getting to Bicocca University

Reaching the university from Sesto San Giovanni is straightforward using Milan's public transport network.

Depending on the faculty or building you're visiting, the journey generally takes around 20 to 30 minutes.

| Starting point | Approximate travel time |
| --- | ---: |
| Sesto San Giovanni | Around 20–30 minutes |
| Duomo | Around 20 minutes |
| Milano Centrale | Around 15–20 minutes |

Travel times may vary depending on your destination within the campus and current public transport services.

## Explore more than the university

If you're staying in Milan for several days, you'll probably want to explore beyond the university campus.

From Sesto San Giovanni you can easily reach many of the city's best-known destinations, including:

- Duomo.
- San Babila.
- Cadorna.
- CityLife.
- Rho Fiera Milano.

The nearby railway station also offers convenient connections to Monza and other destinations across Lombardy, making day trips an easy option.

## Apartment or hotel?

Hotels are a good choice for many travellers, but they're not the only option.

If you're staying for several days, an apartment can offer extra comfort and flexibility.

Many visitors appreciate having:

- More living space.
- A fully equipped kitchen.

- The freedom to organise their day at their own pace.

## Local tip

> Don't choose accommodation based only on the map.

A place that looks slightly further away can actually be easier to reach thanks to a direct metro connection. Spending a few minutes checking your journey before booking can make your entire stay much more comfortable.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it necessary to stay next to Bicocca University?

No. Thanks to Milan's public transport network, many nearby areas offer convenient access to the university while providing a wider range of accommodation.

### Is Sesto San Giovanni a good place to stay when visiting Bicocca?

For many visitors, yes. It combines good public transport, useful local services and a quieter residential atmosphere while remaining well connected to Milan.

### Can I visit Bicocca University without a car?

Absolutely. Most visitors travel to the university using the metro, trains and buses.

If you're staying for several nights or travelling with family, an apartment often provides more space and flexibility than a traditional hotel room.

### Can I easily visit Milan during my stay?

Yes. Staying in a well-connected area makes it easy to combine your university visit with sightseeing and other activities across the city.
