Business travel is often about using your time efficiently.

The right accommodation can reduce travel time, simplify your schedule and make it easier to move between meetings, exhibitions and transport hubs.

Rather than focusing only on the city centre, many professionals choose well-connected neighbourhoods that offer direct metro and railway connections together with a quieter environment after work.

- visiting clients across different parts of the city;
- planning a business stay of several days;

## Choosing the right area

Different business trips have different priorities.

If your meetings are mainly in central Milan, staying close to a metro station usually offers more flexibility than choosing accommodation based only on distance.

If you're attending exhibitions, our guide on [Where to Stay Near Rho Fiera Milano](/guides/where-to-stay-near-rho-fiera-milano) explains why many visitors prefer staying along the **M1 Red Line** instead of booking next to the exhibition centre.

## Why consider Sesto San Giovanni

For many business travellers, **Sesto San Giovanni** offers a practical balance between accessibility and comfort.

The area is connected to central Milan by the M1 Red Line and provides straightforward access to business destinations across the city. It is also useful for visitors travelling to Rho Fiera Milano or working in northern Milan.

If you are unfamiliar with the neighbourhood, our guide on [whether Sesto San Giovanni is a good place to stay](/guides/is-sesto-san-giovanni-a-good-place-to-stay) explains what to expect from the area.

Compared with Milan's busiest central districts, Sesto San Giovanni offers a quieter residential environment with restaurants, cafés, supermarkets and everyday services within walking distance.

For multi-day trips, having these services nearby can make your stay easier and provide a more comfortable place to work or relax after meetings.

## Business trips beyond central Milan

Not every business trip is limited to Milan city centre.

From Sesto San Giovanni, regional railway connections make it convenient to reach **Monza**, Brianza and other destinations across Lombardy.

This can be useful if your schedule includes appointments in several cities or if you plan to extend your visit after your work commitments.

Travellers with free time may also find inspiration in our guide to the [best day trips from Milan by train](/guides/best-day-trips-from-milan-by-train).

## Local tip

> Choose predictable journeys over the shortest distance on a map.

A direct metro route is often more useful than staying closer to your destination but needing several changes.

Before booking, identify the places you need to reach most often and compare the complete journey, including walking time and transfers.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is the city centre always the best place to stay for business travel?

Not necessarily. A well-connected residential area can offer simpler journeys, quieter surroundings and better conditions for longer stays.

### Is the M1 Red Line useful for business travellers?

Yes. It connects Sesto San Giovanni with central Milan and Rho Fiera Milano, while also providing interchange opportunities with other metro lines.

### Is Sesto San Giovanni convenient for Rho Fiera Milano?

Yes. The M1 provides a direct journey without changing trains. Our dedicated guide explains [where to stay near Rho Fiera Milano](/guides/where-to-stay-near-rho-fiera-milano) in more detail.

### Why choose an apartment instead of a hotel?

Apartments can provide more space, privacy and flexibility, particularly for stays of several nights or for travellers who need a comfortable place to work.

### Good to know

Public transport routes, service frequency and travel times may change.

Before travelling, check current information from the relevant transport operator, particularly if you have an early meeting, an evening event or a fixed exhibition schedule.

During major trade fairs and conferences, accommodation availability can become limited, so booking earlier than usual may provide more choice.
