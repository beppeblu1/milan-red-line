# Best Area to Stay in Milan for Business Travellers

Business trips are often about making the most of your time.

Choosing the right area to stay can help you spend less time travelling, avoid unnecessary stress and make it easier to move between meetings, events and transport hubs.

Rather than focusing only on the city centre, many business travellers benefit from staying in a well-connected area with quick access to Milan's public transport network.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide may be helpful if you're:

- Visiting Milan for meetings.
- Attending a trade fair or exhibition.
- Working with clients across different parts of the city.
- Looking for an apartment instead of a hotel.
- Travelling for several days.

## What business travellers usually need

Business travellers often have different priorities from tourists.

A convenient stay usually means:

- Reliable public transport.
- Easy access to railway stations.
- Straightforward connections to business districts.
- Restaurants and cafés nearby.
- Supermarkets for longer stays.
- A quiet place to relax or work after the day is over.

Choosing the right location can make your schedule easier to manage throughout your stay.

## Areas worth considering

Different parts of Milan suit different types of business travel.

If your meetings are mainly in the city centre, staying close to a metro station can make travelling straightforward.

If you're attending exhibitions at Rho Fiera Milano, staying along the M1 Red Line offers a direct connection without changing trains.

For visitors working in northern Milan or moving between several locations, a residential area with good transport links can often be more practical than staying in the busiest central districts.

## Why transport matters more than distance

When travelling for work, efficiency matters.

A hotel in the city centre isn't always the quickest option if reaching your meetings requires multiple transport changes or long walks.

Many experienced travellers prefer accommodation that offers simple, reliable journeys rather than the shortest distance on a map.

Choosing a well-connected neighbourhood can make the entire trip smoother, leaving more time to focus on work instead of logistics.

## Why Sesto San Giovanni is worth considering

For many business travellers, Sesto San Giovanni offers a practical balance between accessibility and comfort.

Located on the M1 Red Line, it provides direct access to central Milan and convenient connections to destinations such as Rho Fiera Milano.

At the same time, the area offers a quieter residential environment where it's easier to unwind after a busy day.

Visitors will also find a wide range of everyday services within walking distance, including:

- Supermarkets.
- Pharmacies.
- Cafés.
- Restaurants.
- Bakeries.
- Local shops.

For longer business stays, having these amenities nearby can make everyday life much more convenient.

## Business trips beyond Milan

Not every business trip is limited to Milan itself.

Thanks to its railway connections, Sesto San Giovanni also provides convenient access to other important destinations across Lombardy, including Monza and Brianza.

This can be particularly useful if your schedule includes meetings in different cities or if you're extending your stay after work commitments.

## Local tip

> Business trips are often easier when your journey is predictable.

Choosing accommodation with a direct metro connection can help reduce travel time, avoid unnecessary changes and make it easier to plan your day with confidence.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is staying in the city centre always the best option for business travellers?

Not necessarily. A well-connected location outside the busiest areas can often provide quicker and more predictable journeys.

### Is the M1 Red Line useful for business travel?

Yes. It connects central Milan with important destinations including Rho Fiera Milano and offers convenient interchange opportunities with the rest of the metro network.

### Why do some business travellers choose apartments instead of hotels?

Apartments often provide more space, greater flexibility and the option to prepare meals, making them particularly suitable for longer stays.

### Is Sesto San Giovanni a good base for business trips?

For many visitors, yes. It combines excellent public transport connections with a quieter residential atmosphere and a wide range of local services.

### Can I reach Milan's main railway stations easily?

Yes. Milan's metro network makes it easy to connect with major railway stations such as Milano Centrale and Cadorna.
