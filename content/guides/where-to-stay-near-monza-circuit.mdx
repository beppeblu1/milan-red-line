# Where to Stay Near Monza Circuit

If you're visiting Monza Circuit, choosing the right place to stay can make your trip more comfortable from the moment you arrive.

Whether you're attending the Formula 1 Italian Grand Prix, another motorsport event or simply visiting the famous Autodromo, staying somewhere with good transport connections can save time and make travelling much easier.

Many visitors immediately look for accommodation in Monza itself. While that can be a good option, it's not the only one worth considering.

A well-connected area nearby can often offer more choice, a quieter environment and easy access to both Monza and Milan.

## Who is this guide for?

This guide may be helpful if you're:

- Attending the Formula 1 Italian Grand Prix.
- Visiting Monza Circuit for another motorsport event.
- Taking part in a track day.
- Combining Monza with a visit to Milan.
- Looking for an apartment instead of a hotel.

## About Monza Circuit

The Autodromo Nazionale Monza is one of the world's most historic racing circuits and has hosted the Formula 1 Italian Grand Prix for decades.

Located inside the Royal Park of Monza, it attracts visitors throughout the year for racing events, driving experiences and other activities.

During major events, accommodation close to the circuit is often in high demand.

For this reason, many visitors choose to stay in nearby areas with convenient transport connections instead of searching only within Monza itself.

## What to look for when choosing accommodation

When comparing different areas, consider more than just the distance to the circuit.

A comfortable stay often depends on practical factors such as:

- Reliable public transport.
- Easy road or railway connections.
- Restaurants and cafés nearby.
- Supermarkets and pharmacies.
- A quiet place to relax after a busy day.

Choosing accommodation that also makes it easy to visit Milan can add even more value to your trip.

## Why Sesto San Giovanni is worth considering

Sesto San Giovanni offers a practical location for visitors who want to combine Monza with Milan during the same trip.

Located between the two cities, it provides convenient access to both while offering a quieter residential environment than many central areas.

The neighbourhood also offers everything most travellers need for a comfortable stay, including:

- Supermarkets.
- Pharmacies.
- Cafés.
- Restaurants.
- Bakeries.
- Everyday services.

For visitors staying several nights, having these amenities within walking distance can make the trip more enjoyable.

## Getting to Monza Circuit

Depending on your preferred route and the event you're attending, Monza Circuit can be reached from Sesto San Giovanni using public transport or by car.

During major events such as the Formula 1 Italian Grand Prix, public transport is often the most practical option, as road traffic and parking around the circuit can become very busy.

Before travelling, it's always worth checking the latest transport information and any special services operating for the event.

## Beyond race weekend

From Sesto San Giovanni you can easily explore:

This makes the area a practical base for visitors who want to make the most of their trip.

## Local tip

> If you're attending a major event at Monza Circuit, don't underestimate travel time.

Large crowds can affect both road traffic and public transport, particularly on race weekends. Leaving a little earlier than planned usually makes the journey much more relaxed.
