# # Milan Red Line — Foundations

## Our Goal

Build a premium website that inspires trust, generates direct bookings and offers an outstanding guest experience.

The website is the official home of Milan Red Line and should become a long-term asset for the business.

---

# Core Principles

## Simplicity

Choose the simplest solution that solves the problem well.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

Every page should have a clear purpose.

---

## Data before Code

The website displays data, it does not contain data.

Apartment information belongs in structured data files (and eventually in a database), not inside components.

---

## User Experience

Every design decision should improve the guest experience.

The website should answer questions before guests need to ask them.

Navigation should be intuitive and distractions kept to a minimum.

---

## Direct Booking First

The primary objective of the website is to encourage direct bookings.

Every important decision should help visitors feel confident enough to contact us directly.

Airbnb and other booking platforms are valuable showcases, but the website is our primary communication channel.

---

## Privacy

The exact apartment address is never displayed publicly.

Only the general area, nearby metro station and approximate location are shown.

The complete address is communicated only after booking.

---

## Transparency

Legal information must always be available.

Each apartment page clearly displays:

- CIN

- CIR (when applicable)

Guests should always know who they are booking with.

---

## Quality over Quantity

A few excellent pages are better than many average ones.

Every photo, every paragraph and every section should exist for a reason.

---

## Consistency

The website should feel like a single coherent product.

Typography, spacing, colours and language should remain consistent across every page.

---

## Documentation

Important decisions are documented.

The project should remain understandable even after several months.

Future improvements should build on documented decisions rather than personal memory.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Milan Red Line is not just a website.

It is the beginning of a recognisable hospitality brand focused on high-quality apartments connected to Milan's Red Line.

The project should be able to grow naturally over time while preserving simplicity, quality and trust.
